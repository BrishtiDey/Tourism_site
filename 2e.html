Bidla Platinum Kolkata

Bidla Platinum is a luxurious residential complex located in the heart of Kolkata. It offers a range of high-end apartments and amenities, making it a popular choice for those seeking luxury living in the city. The complex is located in the bustling neighborhood of Alipore, which is known for its rich history and cultural heritage.

Features and Amenities

Bidla Platinum offers a range of features and amenities to its residents, including:

  • 24-hour security and surveillance
  • Swimming pool
  • Fitness center
  • Clubhouse
  • Indoor games room
  • Landscaped gardens
  • Children's play area
  • Convenience store
  • Power backup

Types of Apartments

Bidla Platinum offers a range of apartment sizes and configurations to suit the needs of different residents. Some of the types of apartments available include:

  • 2 BHK (1,267 sq.ft.)
  • 3 BHK (1,753 sq.ft.)
  • 4 BHK (2,773 sq.ft.)
  • Duplex (2,798 sq.ft.)
  • Penthouse (5,075 sq.ft.)

Location

Bidla Platinum is located in Alipore, one of the most affluent and exclusive neighborhoods in Kolkata. The area is known for its greenery, wide roads, and grand old mansions. The complex is well-connected to the rest of the city, with easy access to public transportation, shopping centers, and entertainment options.
